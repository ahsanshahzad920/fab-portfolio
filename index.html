Mindset, wellbeing, and culture for generational power.

Fabrice Omankoy is a financial professional with over 10 years of experience, bringing knowledge, insight, and a people focused approach to everything he does.

He is also the founder of Black Wealth Connect, a platform dedicated to promoting financial literacy and building generational wealth within the African diaspora. Through Black Wealth Connect, Fabrice empowers communities with the knowledge and tools to take control of their financial future.

His passion for finance started with a childhood fascination with property and wealth creation, and it continues to drive him today. Fabrice is committed to helping individuals and communities turn financial knowledge into real world results.

Success Start With Mindset

Mindset Is Everything by Fabrice Omankoy shares his story of growing up and how his upbringing shaped the way he thinks about money.

The book shows how the way we think influences the way we live, especially when it comes to finances. By shifting your mindset and feeding your mind the right information, you can begin to see opportunities, make better decisions, and take control of your financial life.

At its core, the book is about understanding your financial world, thinking differently, and using knowledge to elevate yourself and your community.
